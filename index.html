Our Story

At charity: water, we believe in a world where everyone has access to clean and safe drinking water. Our mission is rooted in hope, local leadership, and sustainable impact. From deep wells to rainwater catchments, every project brings lasting change. We are providing clean water to rural communities in need by building water projects and educating/promoting locals on the maintenance of these projects long-term.

Real Impact: Meet Amina

Amina used to walk miles every day to collect water for her family. Thanks to your support, her village now has a clean water well. Amina can go to school and her family is healthier and happier.
